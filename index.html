About Fridge


San Francisco is missing 29 million meals per year, while Marin is missing 9 million meals per year.

Fridge aims to bring those numbers down by redistributing leftover food from private events, tech company lunches, grocery stores, and any other situation where pre-packaged food will go to waste.
